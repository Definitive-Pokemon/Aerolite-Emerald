# Pokémon Astral Emerald

**This is a decompilation of Pokémon Modern Emerald, edited to be "Pokémon Astral Emerald".**

As you all know, Pokémon Emerald has received a boatload of enhancement hacks over the years. All of them truly manage to improve upon the original Generation 3 game, but fall short in some way or another: 
- Recharged Emerald stays conservative and lacks an open-source approach to things.
- Emerald Legacy is a great improvement to vanilla in terms of story and Trainer teams, but lacks modern features like the Physical/Special Split.
- Emerald Seaglass looks amazing to some, but terrible to others.
- Finally, Modern Emerald adds a huge amount of quality of life features, remains conservative with other changes (Physical/Special split is an option, but all enemy Pokémon movesets remain unchanged from vanilla Emerald).

Pokémon Astral Emerald takes off to new heights. Add to this an all-new post-game set on the Sevii Islands, and Astral Emerald will surely be the best way to experience Pokémon's Hoenn region.
